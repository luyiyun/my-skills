```markdown
# 2025-03-22 英语学习

## 今日内容

### 词汇 1: Align
- **含义**: 使一致，协调
- **英文释义**: to place or arrange things in a straight line, or to be in the same line; to make something match or agree with something else
- **例句**:
  1. We need to align our goals with the company's vision.
  2. Make sure the text is aligned to the left.
- **常见搭配**: align with, align on, in alignment, strategic alignment

### 词汇 2: Touch base
- **含义**: 简短沟通，跟进
- **例句**: Let's touch base next week to discuss the progress.
```

```markdown
## 2025-03-24 - 现在完成时 vs 过去时

### 规则说明
- **现在完成时**: 过去动作对现在有影响，或从过去持续到现在
- **过去时**: 单纯描述过去发生的动作，与现在无关

### 例句对比
| 现在完成时 | 过去时 |
|-----------|--------|
| I have lost my keys. (现在还没找到) | I lost my keys yesterday. (单纯陈述) |
| She has lived here for 5 years. (现在还住) | She lived here in 2020. (现在不住) |

### 常见错误
❌ I have seen that movie last week.
✅ I saw that movie last week. / I have seen that movie.

❌ Did you ever visit Paris?
✅ Have you ever visited Paris?
```

```markdown
The COVID-19 pandemic changed how we work. Before 2020, working from home was rare. Now, many companies offer hybrid or fully remote options...

[全文内容]

### 生词
- **hybrid**: 混合的
- **commute**: 通勤
- **flexibility**: 灵活性

### 理解题

1. What changed how we work according to the article?
   - Answer: The COVID-19 pandemic.

2. What does "hybrid" mean in this context?
   - Answer: A mix of working from home and working in the office.

3. What is one benefit of remote work mentioned?
   - Answer: No commute time.

### 个人笔记
- 学到了 hybrid 的新用法
- 文章结构：现象 → 原因 → 影响
```
